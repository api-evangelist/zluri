The article argues that employee lifecycle management focuses heavily on onboarding and offboarding while neglecting mover events such as promotions and department transfers, which create unmanaged access accumulation. Most organizations handle these with a Slack message, a manual ticket, or a quiet hope that someone follows up, leading to privilege creep over time.
