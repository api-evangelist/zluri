ISPM is the continuous process of discovering identity risk across your environment, evaluating it in context, prioritizing exposures by business impact, and remediating them before they become incidents. Unlike periodic access reviews that reconstruct outdated risk pictures, ISPM monitors posture in real time and surfaces drift as it builds between governance cycles.
